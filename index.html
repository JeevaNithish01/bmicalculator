Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical value derived from an individual's height and weight. It provides an estimate of body fat and serves as a basic gauge for potential health risks associated with body weight.


• Underweight:

Indicates a weight lower than what's generally considered healthy for a given height.

• Healthy Weight:

Suggests a balanced weight concerning height.

• Overweight:

A weight range higher than what's considered healthy for a specific height.

• Obese:

Significantly above the healthy weight range. Being obese can increase the risk of numerous health complications and diseases.
